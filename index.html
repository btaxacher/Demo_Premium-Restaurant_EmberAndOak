The Ethos

A Study in Smoke.

We believe that heat is an ingredient. Stripped of pretense, fine dining returns to its primal roots: fire, wood, and time. In the hushed glow of our dining room, ingredients are coaxed to their absolute limits over open embers of white oak and cherry.

Our cellar houses vintages selected not just for pedigree, but for their ability to stand up to the robust, unapologetic flavors forged in our hearth.
